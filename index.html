About Olea Computer

We're building the foundational infrastructure for a world where autonomous agents operate as trusted economic participants.

Our first product, Sylva, creates a framework where AI agents can be owned, trained, validated, and rewarded on-chain. We believe the future of work involves human-agent collaboration, and we're building the tools to make that future safe, transparent, and economically viable.

Founded in 2024, Olea Computer is focused on the intersection of blockchain technology and autonomous systems.
